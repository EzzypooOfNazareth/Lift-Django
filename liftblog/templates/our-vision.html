Our Vision:

The Spirit of the Lord God is upon me,
Because the Lord has anointed me
to bring the good news to the afflicted;
He has sent me to bind up the brokenhearted,
to proclaim liberty to captives
and freedom to prisoners;
To proclaim the favorable year of the Lord
and the day of vengence of our God;
To comfort all who mourn,
to grant those who mourn in Zion,
giving them garland instead of ashes,
the oil of gladness instead of mourning,
the mantle of praise instead of a spirit of fainting.
So they will be called oaks of righteousness,
the planting of the Lord, that He may glorify

- Isaiah 61:1-3

  • L: Liberty:
  • To proclaim liberty-freedom to captives and freedom to prisoners; (Isa 61:1)


  • I: International:
  • The message will be sent out to the nations; (Acts 1:8)


  • F: Fellowship:
  • Developing of relationships and devotion; (Acts 2:42)


  • T: Transformation:
  • Changing the hearts of people; (2nd Cor 3:8, Rom 12:1-2)
