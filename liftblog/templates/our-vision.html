Our Vision: L.I.F.T.

  • L - Liberty:
  • Proclaiming we are a people of liberty-freedom in every aspect of church life. Freedom to dance, sing, shout, lifting of hands, banners, sit, stand, kneel, cry, laugh etc. A place where we allow Holy Spirit and Gods Word to have their way. A place where we celebrate the expression and love of God-particularly in worship.

    Isaiah: 61:1-3, 1 Tim 2:8, Pslam 30 11-12, Luke 19:37-41, II Cor. 3:17


  • I - International:
  • The message of the gospel is to be spread throughout all the world-its international. First to our local community- Secondly, to the Nation - Lastly, to All Nations. The message of the gospel is for all people and people groups.

    Acts 1:8, Mark 16:14-18


  • F - Fellowship:
  • Developing relationships with one another (fellowship) and encouraging one another in our devotion to God. We need one another.

    Acts: 2:42


  • T - Transformation:
  • The Word of God-Holy Spirit are interwoven in the believer and are not to be separated. They have the power to transform and change our lives from a sinner to saint. God is able to transform any situation for His Glory.

    II Corinthians 2:3-8, Romans 12: 1-2, Colossians 1:13-14
