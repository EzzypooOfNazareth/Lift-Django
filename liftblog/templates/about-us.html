Gerard Barral is a graduate of Zion Bible Institue-Township of Barrington as well as the founder and lead pastor for LIFT.

Mary Barral is a graduate of Zion Bible Institue-Township of Barrington as well as the treasurer and teacher.

Founded in 2013 by Gerard and Mary Barral. The Barral's are graduates of the Zion Bible Institue-Township of Barrington, State of Rhode Island under the leadership of Dr. Benjamin Crandall and Dean, Patrick Gallagher. Class of 2000. Both hold their Bachelor's degrees in Biblical Theology and Minors in Pastoral Ministries.

In addition, they are proud parents of Victoria, Junior at Berklee's College of Music, and Samuel, Senior with Honors and Distinction as Dallas-Senior High School. Gerard and Mary have been married for 21 years, a real life experience. In addition to their children, they love their dog, Oreo.

It is their joy to spread the message of the gospel of The Lord, Jesus Christ and His Kingdom. The areas of ministerial focus will be multifaceted as express in their mission, vision, and core values of LIFT Church.

With the mindset and spirit of an entrepreneur, Gerard also formed his own company in 2017 - We Got the Power LLC. which is currently in successful operation.
